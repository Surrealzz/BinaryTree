Empowering Kids Through Coding

We are a nonprofit dedicated to teaching programming skills to kids in Africa!

Our Mission and Activities

Our Goal
To provide programming education to children in Africa.
Current Activities
Organizing fundraisers and designing social media campaigns to raise awareness.
How to Help
Participate in our events, donate, or spread the word about our cause.

Our mission is to empower the next generation by equipping kids in Africa with essential programming skills. Through dedicated fundraisers and social media campaigns, we aim to increase awareness and support for this critical cause. Join us in making a difference and helping kids unlock their potential through coding.
